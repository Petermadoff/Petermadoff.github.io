What you need:

One .25in x 2ft x 4ft piece of birch plywood, one jigsaw, wood glue, 12 clamps, 2 longboard trucks, 2 pairs of longboard wheels, and 1 Set of Steel Skate Bearings

The Process:

Step 1: Cut your wood into three 8 inch wide strips
Step 2: Glue all three steps on top of one another. At the end you should have one three layer strip of ply wood with glue in between each layer
Step 3: Place the 12 clamps on either end of the board evenly spaced out to hold the board together while it dries
Step 4: Wait 3ish hours for the glue to dry


Step 5: If the board is dry, trace the general outline of your longboard in pencil and cut along that outline with your jigsaw
Step 6: Drill in holes at either end of the board for the trucks
Step 7: Sand down the board so it is smooth on all sidese
Step 8: Add trucks and grip tape to board
